> What is the command to stop/exit the fbsplash daemon?

/usr/local/sbin/fbsplashd -t default -p 10000 -q --type=bootup
--pidfile="$PIDFILE"

To stop the daemon just send it "exit" command:

PIPE=/lib/splash/cache/.splash
echo "exit" > "$PIPE"
